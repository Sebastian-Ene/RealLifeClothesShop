Purpose

The purpose of this web application is to simulate a store.

Components

Although these are some difficult times, this store has customers. Each customer has a first name, a last name and a list of products. What he doesn't know is that we know the exact total price of the items he's chosen.

There are products in this store. Jeans, t-shirts and dresses. Each product has a color, price and size.

Of course there are dressing rooms. Three of those. They can be either occupied or free. A customer can not use a dressing room if he has more than 3 products. Or if all the dressing rooms are full. Who would've guessed?
